Chatura Suraksha Kavacha - The smart safety helmet for mine workers

Chatura Suraksha Kavacha - final year B.E. academic project A cutting-edge IoT-based smart helmet designed to ensure the safety of mine workers. This helmet is equipped with multiple sensors to detect hazardous gases, monitor environmental conditions, and provide real-time surveillance using an ESP32-CAM. In case of emergencies, an integrated alert system notifies supervisors instantly, ensuring a prompt response. The helmet also prevents unauthorized removal and continuously tracks worker location for enhanced safety.

Features

  • Real-time hazardous gas detection (Methane, CO, LPG, etc.)
  • Continuous worker monitoring using ESP32-CAM
  • Emergency button for instant alert to supervisors
  • Helmet removal detection with immediate notifications
  • Temperature and humidity monitoring for workplace safety
  • Wireless data transmission using NodeMCU and Blynk IoT
  • Low-cost, efficient, and easy to maintain

Highlights

  • Enhanced safety for mine workers with IoT integration
  • Smart alerts via Blynk app for gas detection and helmet removal
  • Live video streaming from helmet-mounted ESP32-CAM
  • Fast, reliable, and long-range wireless communication
  • Designed to prevent workplace hazards and improve worker safety
  • Participated/Exhibited in KSCST-2023 and Won"KSCST - 2023 Project of the year award"
